Article 1

The Transferee, the schools that comprise it, and their agents, representatives, or delegates produce or commission audiovisual content within the framework of the themes and uses described above, in which the Transferor appears or may appear.

Article 2

The audiovisual content consists of photographs, videos, and sound recordings made by any capturing means, intended to be displayed, reproduced, and exploited in any form and on any medium, including but not limited to press, internet, video, publishing, mailings, catalogs, flyers, brochures, press kits, displays, etc.

Article 3

The Transferee undertakes to limit the use of the audiovisual content to the uses described herein, or to an institutional use in order to illustrate or promote the current, past, or future activities or projects of HES-SO Genève or the schools that comprise it, and exclusively in a context directly related to them.

Article 4

The Transferee may make any adaptation, modification, and/or cropping of the audiovisual content according to the needs of its communication or its media.

Article 5

The Transferor grants the Transferee, free of charge, the entirety of their image rights for the entire duration of the legal protection of the Transferor's image and worldwide. In this regard:

  1. They give their consent to the capture of their image and/or voice.
  2. They authorize the public dissemination of their image and/or voice within the framework defined in Articles 2, 3, and 4.
  3. They authorize, if necessary, the publication of their first and last name or course of study, limited to any accompanying text to the image.

Article 6

The Transferor acknowledges that the use of the audiovisual content within the framework defined in Articles 2, 3, and 4 is not likely to infringe upon their privacy, or more generally, to harm them or cause any prejudice.

Article 7

This authorization may be withdrawn at any time by informing the Transferee in writing.

This contract is governed exclusively by Swiss law. The jurisdiction is in Geneva.
